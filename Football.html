Best Goals of the Weekend (based on 18 April)

"Pure. Football. Magic."

In the 79th minute, Lionel Messi seized a midfield turnover and cut inside the penalty area to break a 2-2 deadlock. He unleashed a clinical, rising strike that split two defenders and tucked perfectly into the far post. The goal secured a 3-2 victory for Inter Miami, completing Messi’s brace in front of a record Denver crowd.

Ander Barrenetxea made history by scoring just 14 seconds into the Copa del Rey final, shattering a 74-year-old record with a clinical header. This lightning-fast strike ignited a classic showdown that ultimately saw Real Sociedad lift the trophy after a dramatic penalty shootout (Real Sociedad won 4-3). It was a legendary moment that cemented Barrenetxea’s place in Spanish football folklore and defined an unforgettable night for the fans.
